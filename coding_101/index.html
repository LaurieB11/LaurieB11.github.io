My Greece Trip

October 2018

We spent 7 days there and it was not nearly enough! I would gladly go back to visit again, next time for much longer!

There are so many things to do:
  • Hike one of the stunning gorges in Crete
  • See the ancient ruins in Athens
  • Sit on the water and eat fresh seafood in Hydra
  • Wander through the streets filled with brightly painted houses and shops
  • Chat with locals in Chania about their favorite spots
